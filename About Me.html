Hello World


I would like to welcome everyone to my blog. I like to travel and meet new people. I also love trying new food and listening to new music. I will use this space to add into my findings.

And you ... why do you travel?

All this has given me a lot to think about, reflecting on the reasons why I travel.

And probably the headline would come here given by the name of our blog, Traveler Learning. I travel mostly to learn, to surprise me.

To be able to understand what is beyond the imaginary line that we invent humans and call border. To be able to see closely the stories and adventures that I always read in novels or with which I enjoyed in the cinema.
