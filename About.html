How long can you go around the world?

Ok, this is something personal. I understand that in nine months you can travel through Spain (much of it) and learn Spanish.

I understand that nine months is enough to understand many cultures that can be shared within a continent. Several related countries that share several characteristics where you can really soak up things and get a general idea.

I understand that you can see Southeast Asia in nine months, that you can travel across much of South America at that time.

But the world is too big.

An entrepreneur of a multinational has gone around the world infinitely.

Does it mean that you have "traveled"?

Maybe, maybe.

Our time is short. Let's enjoy it.
