The result of a few years of backpacking, these country travel guides are the collection of personal advice and experiences with the sole purpose of assisting the independent traveler in planning.

Malaysia

Wild nature and dream beaches in the same country, in which the varied cuisine will satisfy even the most demanding palate.

Myanmar (or Burma)

Opens up to tourism by leaps and bounds showing the world that it has a lot to offer beyond Bagan temples or millions of Buddha images.

Singapore

The huge city characterized by its mix of cultures and its neighborhoods. More expensive than its neighboring countries but with many reasons to visit it.

Vietnam

Fascinating from north to south and offers all kinds of activities: trekking through the Sapa Valley, sailing through Halong Bay, wander through ancient citadels or getting lost in the canals of the Mekong Delta. Do you dare to ride it on a motorcycle?
