Preparing to go home...

The return home, the day you never want to arrive

In the end we go home, of course, and to finish off the trip we realize that we don't have the keys and we still have to go to pick up a copy that our parents have. We can no longer... close your eyes and think: next year they will be more.
There are times when you finish a tired trip, wanting to go home, sit on your sofa, sleep in your bed ... but 18 days abroad are not enough to make you miss all that, less even if you have waited so long.
